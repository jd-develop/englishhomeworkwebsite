Who are we ?

We are a Kenya-passionned team who thinks that preserving this country is very important for nature and for future generations.

The Kenya is a beautiful country where there is a huge and important biodiversity.

In 2016, we decided to create an association to make some actions. You can help us by donating!

Our projects

Beach preserving and cleaning

Kenya has a lot of beautiful beaches. But because of ocean pollution and tourism, some beaches are covered by plastics.

To avoid this, we inform the tourists about the danger of pollution on the ocean wildlife. We put signs on the beach that requests to respect nature.

But, frequently, this is not enough. In this case, we clean the beaches by removing these plastics with volunteers.

All the plastics are sent to an association that recycle them into pieces of art. It's beautiful, creative and creates jobs!

 

Protecting savana wildlife

In the Kenya savana, there is a lot of emblematic animals like lions, giraffes, elephants...

But these animals are in danger of extinction because of global warming and poachers.

Our association, with the help of the Maasai tribes, helps the Kenyan government to track the poachers and to catch them.

We also informate people about the fact that these species are in danger of extinction, and that they should protect them.
